Information

The ZFS on Linux mailing lists are available for anyone for wants to get involved. There is an annoucement list if you just want to be notified on new releases, a discussion list for user issues/concerns, and a development list for technical discussion.

To subscribe to a list you can use one of three methods:

  • Enter your email address to the right of the list above and click 'Subscribe'.
  • Send an email to <list-name>+subscribe@zfsonlinux.org.
  • If you have a Google account you can use their web interface.

To unsubscribe from a list you can either:

  • Send an email to <list-name>+unsubscribe@zfsonlinux.org.
  • If you have a Google account you can use their web interface.
